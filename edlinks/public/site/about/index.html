About the Site

Hello! This site contains links to useful resources for education training providers in the UK.

It is not owned or operated by any department or office of the UK government, although it does borrow some styles from the GOV.UK Design System.

My name is Luke Burns, and I made this site (along with a few others). If you have any questions or suggestions you can use the contact form here: Contact Us.
